Treasure hunt is a game created by a group of students (THE_RAIN) and its purpose is to create a treasure hunt type of game for students and others to play within the area of UCLan Cyprus!

If you need help using the game, these are the instructions:
  • Click the Let's get started button!
  • Select which Treasure Hunt you want to Follow!
  • Pick your team's username!
  • Answer the questions!
  • If you don't know the answer to a question, just skip
  • If the question can not be skipped, seek for help!
  • Finally, get your final Score and Follow us on Social Media!
